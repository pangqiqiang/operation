zookeeper的监控方式主要有2种.
The ZooKeeper service can be monitored in one of two primary ways; 1) the command port through the use of 4 letter words and 2) JMX. See the appropriate section for your environment/requirements.
1.zookeeper命令行模式结合telnet或者nc:http://zookeeper.apache.org/doc/r3.4.6/zookeeperAdmin.html#sc_zkCommands
2.通过JMX监控zookeeper:http://zookeeper.apache.org/doc/r3.4.6/zookeeperJMX.html
